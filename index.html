Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the mobile application "Critical Calligraphy Chanting" published by Motoki Furusawa.

Information Collection

This app does not collect, store, or use any personally identifiable information from users.

However, the app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements, which may collect certain non-personally identifiable information from your device, such as:

  • Device information (e.g., Android Advertising ID)
  • App usage data
  • Location data (if enabled by the user)

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob uses the collected data to serve relevant ads and enhance user experience. Data collected by AdMob is managed in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy and Google’s Advertising Policies.

Users can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting their device settings or visiting the Google Ads Settings page.

Data Storage

This app stores settings and other data configured by the user only on the user’s device. This data is not shared externally.

Provision to Third Parties

Except for Google AdMob as described above, this app does not share any user information with third parties.

Changes

This privacy policy is subject to change without notice. Any updates will be posted within the app or on the official website.
